Article B

The Pudding article about “How Algorithms Know What You’ll Type Next” has content about algorithms and A.I. like my project does, this is helpful because it shows how content regarding A.I. could function interactively. While most of the information is given through words and numbers rather than graphics, the many small interactive elements makes it so much easier to consume.
